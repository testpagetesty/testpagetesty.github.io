Blackjack Guide

Master the classic card game with our comprehensive guide

Basic Rules

Objective

The objective of blackjack is to get a hand value of 21 or as close to 21 as possible without going over.

Card Values

  • Aces: Valued at 1 or 11 (the value that's most beneficial)
  • Face cards (J, Q, K): Valued at 10 points
  • Number cards: Valued at their face value (2-10)

Game Development

  1. You place your initial bet
  2. You receive two visible cards
  3. The dealer receives one visible and one hidden card
  4. You decide whether:
    • Hit: You receive an additional card
    • Stand: You keep your current cards

Special Rules

  • Natural Blackjack: If your first two cards sum up to 21 (A + 10), you win automatically 1.5 times your bet
  • Dealer's Rule: The dealer must hit on 16 or less and stand on 17 or more

Basic Tips

  • Always hit if you have 11 or less
  • Stand if you have 17 or more
  • With 12-16, depend on the dealer's visible card
